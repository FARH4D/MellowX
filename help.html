The Mellow X

Here at team Bingus we aim to make a positive impact when it comes to mental health and anxiety, while we obviously accept that it is a hard task for a small team like ours, we hope to slowly shorten the gap and make the best impact possible :)

The Mellow X isn't a device that magically cures your anxiety and mental health, but it is a device that slowly helps YOU to better yourself and identify the causes of your struggles.

What you are viewing today is just a prototype and can change massively with more development time and a higher budget.

How does the Mellow X work?

The Mellow X hosts this website that you can use to state your current problem, the Mellow X will then offer you possible reasons and solutions for your problem so you can try to slowly fix them one step at a time.

Our product also has a heart rate sensor and an air quality sensor, these two modules are used to further identify any possible causes of your problems (high resting heart rate or high room humidity).

If these causes are detected, the Mellow X will warn you before advising and teaching you about what to do next.
